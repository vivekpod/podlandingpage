Eligibility

10+2 (Any Board, Any Group, Any Age) + Based in India (to be able to take exams) + Should have been admitted to college or should have graduated. Those who are eligible may REGISTER for the Qualifying Process.

Qualifying Process

4 COURSES | 4 WEEKS of content and assignments for each course. QUALIFYING EXAM at the end of 4 weeks. Those who clear the QUALIFYING EXAM will be eligible for the FOUNDATIONAL PROGRAMME.

Foundational Programme

Eligibility: Successfully clearing QUALIFYING EXAM .8 COURSES | Minimum 2 terms (or 8 months) Complete all 8 courses to become FOUNDATION CERTIFIED from Centre for Continuing Education, IIT Madras.

Diploma Programme

Eligibility: FOUNDATION CERTIFIED or LATERAL ENTRY 12 COURSES | Minimum 3 terms (or 12 months) Complete all 12 courses to get DIPLOMA IN PROGRAMMING & DATA SCIENCE from Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM). Option to get DIPLOMA IN PROGRAMMING or DIPLOMA IN DATA SCIENCE upon completing 6 courses.

Degree Programme

Eligibility: DIPLOMA IN PROGRAMMING & DATA SCIENCE Minimum 11 COURSES | Minimum 3 terms (or 12 months) Three specialisations to pick from: COMPUTER SYSTEMS COMPUTER APPLICATIONS ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE & MACHINE LEARNING Complete all 11 courses to get BSC IN PROGRAMMING & DATA SCIENCEfrom Indian Institute of Technology Madras, and become an IIT MADRAS ALUMNUS

Course Format

Enrollment: There are 3 terms per year and a student can enroll for a minimum 2 courses and a maximum of 6 courses per term assuming they meet the prerequisites of each course they wish to enroll in. A student may choose to skip a term upon paying a nominal fee to keep the registration afloat.

Course Content: Each course content runs for a total of 12 weeks with about 2.5 hours of video content released every Monday. Assignments and reading material for each course vary from week to week.

Assessments: There will be 3 proctored quizzes at the end of 5th, 8th, 11th week and one final exam, along with a number of assignments during the term. For the calculations of the quiz score, best 2 out of 3 will be taken. The learner will have to score a minimum of 50% overall and at least 40% in the final exam to clear the course.

Prerequisites/Eligibility

Should be based in India to take the proctored exams. Studied Mathematics and English until 10th standard or Equivalent. Passed XII (or Equivalent) or 3 year Diploma Program. Graduated (Degree or Diploma), or pursuing an undergraduate degree programme or Dropped off a degree programme.

Qualifying Process

All registered candidates, meeting eligibility criteria, will have access to the first 4 weeks of content. Program enrollment will be based on their performance in the qualification quiz, which will be a proctored exam, conducted at the end of it.

Tentative Schedule

Registrations for Qualifier Exam: 1st June - 30th June
Qualifier Courses: 1st June - 31st July
Qualifier Exam: 9th August / 10th August / 16th August / 17th August
Qualifier Exam Results: 31st August
FOR THOSE WHO ARE SELECTED FROM THE QUALIFIER EXAM: Enrolment for 1st Semester: 1st September - 13th September
1st Semester Begins: 14th September
